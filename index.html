Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 20/20/20 rule help my eyes?

The rule helps relax the focusing muscles in your eyes and reduces digital eye strain by giving your eyes regular breaks from close-up work.

What are Live Activities?

Live Activities let you see your timer progress directly on your Lock Screen and in the Dynamic Island, so you can stay aware of your break schedule without opening the app.

Does the app work in the background?

EyeTimer is designed to send gentle notifications even when you're using other apps, ensuring you never miss a break.
